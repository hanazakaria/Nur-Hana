Smart Reader Kids


I entered kindergarden at Smart Reader Kids in Cheras. I had lots of friends who are kind and very sporting. Kindergarden life was very fun because we learn many new things and played some games together. Smart Reader Kids uses English most of the time. We rarely use Bahasa Melayu when we are in a learning session. The teachers are very thoughtful and all of them are very nice people. They cared about us like we are their own children. Learning at Smart Reader Kids is an enjoyable experience and you can gain a lot of new knowledge.
